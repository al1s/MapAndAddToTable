My Invoices Pending Approval

List of invoices that require your approval (or rejection) within maximum 72 hours to complete payment out to supplier.

As an approver, you are responsible for verifying the goods and/or services have been provided before approving an invoice for payment. If you delegate approval, invoices assigned to you during the selected period will be re-directed to your delegate. POE (Proof of Execution) must be uploaded before approval for invoices flagged with ‘POE’.

Help Microsoft collect discounts and pay within terms. Need support? Go to P2P Support.

Approval Delegation

When assigning a delegate, please ensure that the delegate has appropriate SAFE limits in case of Non-PO invoices. SAFE Limits can be checked at https://msauthorize.microsoft.com.
